Fresumes - An online recruiting platform

             

I with 4 other team members designed a web application for a buffalo based startup. Fresumes is a platform where recuiters can hunt for best talents among the pool of talent. We designed a resume parser which uses spacy to parse the resume and populates the respective tables. This makes searching and filtering for candidates based on their location, skills, certifications etc very smooth and easy to go. We integrated a subscription plan using stripe where in recruiter can subscribe to buying fresume credits and use the credits to send mass or individual email or text messages targeting the individuals of their choice. We made use of Twilio and Courier to implement the communication system. We also designed a state of the art resume builder which the jobseekers could use to build their resumes and store it in the platform or share it with others. We made use AWS services such as AWS Lambda and S3 to store the resumes.
